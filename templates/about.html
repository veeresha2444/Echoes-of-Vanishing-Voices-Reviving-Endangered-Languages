About Our Platform

Learn more about our mission to preserve and teach Konkani and Lambani languages using modern AI technology.

Our Mission

We believe that language preservation and learning should be engaging, accessible, and effective. Our platform was created to help people learn Konkani and Lambani through modern, interactive methods that make the process enjoyable and rewarding while preserving these beautiful languages for future generations.

Our Technology

We use state-of-the-art machine learning models, specifically fine-tuned BART transformers, to provide accurate translations between Hindi and Konkani. Our AI-powered system continuously learns and improves to deliver better translation quality.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, our platform grew from a passion for preserving Indian regional languages. What started as a capstone project has now evolved into a comprehensive learning platform serving language enthusiasts worldwide.
